Project Title : Customer Churn Analysis and Prediction

Short Description / Purpose
This report involves analyzing customer behavior and historical data to identify patterns that lead to customer attrition, and using predictive models to forecast which customers are likely to churn. This helps businesses take proactive actions to improve retention and reduce revenue loss.
To identify customers at risk of leaving, understand the key factors driving churn, and enable businesses to take proactive actions to improve customer retention and long-term profitability.

Tech Stack
1. Power BI – Designing interactive dashboards to visualize churn trends, KPIs, and predictive insights.
2. SQL – Extracting and managing customer data from databases.
3. Excel – Initial data exploration and validation.
4. Power Query – Data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL)
5. DAX – Creating calculated measures, KPIs, and churn metrics within Power BI

Features 
1. Interactive dashboard with filters for customer segments, plans, and time periods
2. Key churn such as churn rate, retention rate, and customer lifetime value.
3. Visual analysis of churn trends and patterns over time.
4. Customer segmentation based on demographics and usage behavior.
5. Identification of high-risk customers using predictive insights.
6. Clear, business-friendly visualizations to support data-driven decision making.

Highlights 
1. Clear overview of key KPIs including total customers, churn count, tenure, total charges, and average monthly charges.
2. Interactive slicers for Partner status, Contract type, and Gender to enable dynamic analysis.
3. Churn comparison across different contract types (Month-to-month, One year, Two year).
4. Internet service–wise churn distribution (Fiber optic, DSL, No service).
5. Analysis of churn based on dependents and online security status.
6. Combined view of Total Charges by Internet Service, Churn, and Payment Method.
7. Identification of Top 25 customers based on tenure and monthly charges.
8. Visual breakdown of Total Charges by Tenure and Customer ID using treemap.
9. DAX-driven measures and Power Query transformations for accurate, real-time insights.
10.User-friendly layout with actionable insights to support customer retention strategies.
